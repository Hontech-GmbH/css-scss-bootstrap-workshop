01 - Margin Calculation

Write and apply a function on the block/circle that calculates your margin property (top, right, bottom, left, e.g. 50px 0 0 100px).
Block in the small container has a margin-top of 20% of its height and margin-left of 40% of its width.
Circle in the big container has a margin-top of 50% of its height and margin-left of 70% of its width.
